User Need

Most newcomers to University of Michigan have trouble finding classrooms. Apart from the confusing building abbreviations, finding correct buildings as well as classrooms is hard in a big campus.

As the image shown above, it is generally difficult for a new engineering student to tell the exact location of "EECS" and "Dow". Therefore, it is essential to develop an application to help new engineering students find their classrooms more easily.

Problem

The problem our application tries to solve is to help new engineering students find classrooms at North Campus of University of Michigan easily.
  • Help users recognize the appearance of buildings
  • Help users identify building abbreviations
  • Help users memorize routes to different buildings and classrooms
    • Our application, DefendUmich, is a Tower-defense Game based on the map in the North Campus. Users can learn how to get to classrooms while playing our game. Click Features to check out more details!

    Features

    Simple and elegant starting page.

    Easy to understand tutorial.

    First step is to choose the building based on your schedule.

    Multiple buildings have information documented.

    After clicking, view information and choose room.

    Multiple rooms could be chosen correspondingly as base.

    Click on a building and place it on the map with clear indication.

    The placed building will start attacking/frozening the monsters in range.

    Second wave of monsters are faster, plan well :)

    Third wave of monsters are crazy, watch out =P

    Congratulations! You could choose another base to play!

    Oops! Play again to practice more maybe...
