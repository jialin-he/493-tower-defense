Features

Simple and elegant starting page.

Easy to understand tutorial.

First step is to choose the building based on your schedule.

Multiple buildings have information documented.

After clicking, view information and choose room.

Multiple rooms could be chosen correspondingly as base.

Click on a building and place it on the map with clear indication.

The placed building will start attacking/frozening the monsters in range.

Second wave of monsters are faster, plan well :)

Third wave of monsters are crazy, watch out =P

Congratulations! You could choose another base to play!

Oops! Play again to practice more maybe...
