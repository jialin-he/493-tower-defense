Phase 1: Choose Your Base


Hover on the building to see its photo in real life




Click on the building to see its address, zip code and property as a base in the game




Choose a specific room you want to go and click on the button to go to the actual game page or close the modal to choose another base



Phase 2: Defend Your Base


Click on the tower to buy tower and click on an empty spot to place the tower

Note: You are not allowed to place a tower on the road or on any base.




Frozen tower can slow monsters down while attacking them

Hint: Make good use of frozen tower!




Your budget is limited, kill monsters to get money to buy towers




HP of your base is displayed up there, try to keep it green:




Survive that many waves of monster and then YOU WIN!
